About us

From knowing the words to saying them.

Speaki is a spoken English platform for non-native speakers — built on the belief that confidence, not vocabulary, is what's really holding most learners back.

Why we built Speaki

Most people learning English don't have a vocabulary problem. They've studied for years. They can read, write, and understand. But when it's their turn to speak — in a meeting, an interview, a phone call — they freeze. The fear of judgment and the worry about their accent get in the way of words they already know.

Traditional courses and apps double down on the thing learners already have: grammar drills and word lists. Speaki was built to fix the part everyone else skips — the confidence to actually speak.

Our mission

To help you communicate efficiently and confidently in English — so you can unlock better opportunities at work, in interviews, and in life.

Who Speaki is for

If your work speaks but you don't, Speaki was designed for you. Our learners include:

  • Working professionals who want to speak up confidently in meetings, presentations, and 1:1s.
  • Students who want to participate and present without fear of being judged.
  • Job seekers preparing for interviews and networking in English.
  • BPO and call-centre aspirants building clear, confident, customer-facing English.
  • Everyday learners turning years of study into real spoken conversation.

Our approach to accents

We don't believe in accent shaming. Your accent is proof you speak more than one language — a feature, not a flaw. Speaki focuses on clarity and confidence, helping you be understood and feel at ease, not on erasing who you are.
