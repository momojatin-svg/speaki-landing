Speaki, answered.

Everything you might want to know about live classes, mindset coaching, the AI coach, and how to start speaking with confidence.

What is Speaki? +
Speaki is a spoken English learning platform for non-native English speakers. It combines live speaking classes, mindset coaching, an AI speaking coach, and a supportive community to help you speak English with confidence — not just learn more grammar.
Who is Speaki for? +
Working professionals, students, job seekers, BPO and call-centre aspirants, and any non-native speaker who understands English but freezes when it's time to speak. If your work speaks but you don't, Speaki was designed for you.
How is Speaki different from other English apps? +
Most apps drill vocabulary and grammar you likely already have. Speaki focuses on the missing piece — speaking out loud with real people, plus mindset coaching that addresses the fear, freezing, and self-doubt that stop you from speaking.
Is there a free way to try Speaki? +
Yes. You can join a live session for free to experience speaking English with real people and the identity-shift mindset coaching, at no cost. Reserve a free spot here.
What is the AI speaking coach? +
The Speaki AI coach lets you practise speaking English anytime, 24/7, with instant feedback and personalised tips. It's launching soon — join the waitlist for first access.
Do I need a high level of English to start? +
No. Speaki welcomes all levels. Sessions are designed so you can speak in a safe, no-judgment space regardless of your current level — the goal is confidence and clarity, not perfection.
Will Speaki help with my accent? +
Speaki helps you speak clearly and confidently without shame about your accent. The focus is being understood, not erasing your accent — your accent is treated as a feature, not a flaw.
How do I join a session? +
Visit the Speaki homepage and use the "Join a Live Session" form to reserve a free spot, or sign up for the AI coach waitlist for early access.
